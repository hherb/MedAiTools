# MedAiTools User Guide

Welcome to MedAiTools! This guide will help you get started with using the AI-powered medical research workbench.

## What is MedAiTools?

MedAiTools is a suite of AI tools designed to help medical researchers and healthcare professionals:

- **Ask questions about PDF documents** using natural language
- **Find and analyze research papers** from MedRxiv and other sources
- **Generate quality assessments** of research papers
- **Create synthetic patient data** for research and testing
- **Conduct automated literature reviews**

## Key Features

### PDF Librarian
Upload medical research papers and ask questions in natural language. The AI will search through your documents and provide answers with source citations.

### MedRxiv Browser
Browse the latest preprints from MedRxiv. Filter by date, category, and keywords. Download papers automatically for local analysis.

### Research Assistant
Ask a research question and let the AI conduct comprehensive web research, summarize findings, and provide a detailed report with citations.

### Study Critique
Upload a research paper and receive an AI-generated quality assessment, including methodology critique, bias detection, and recommendations.

### Synthetic Data Generator
Generate realistic synthetic patient records for testing healthcare applications without privacy concerns.

## Requirements

- **Operating System:** Windows, macOS, or Linux
- **Python:** 3.8 or higher
- **Memory:** 8GB RAM minimum (16GB recommended)
- **Storage:** 10GB free space
- **Internet:** Required for cloud LLM providers (optional for local models)

## Privacy

MedAiTools is designed with privacy in mind:

- **Local-first:** All data stays on your computer by default
- **No tracking:** We don't collect any usage data
- **Your choice:** You control which AI providers to use
- **Open source:** You can verify exactly what the software does
